Currently, I'm focused on enhancing my design skills, particularly in UI and problem-solving, while also working on becoming a better programmer. I'm reading "JavaScript: The Definitive Guide" to deepen my understanding of this powerful programming language.


When I'm not designing or coding, you'll likely find me traveling to new countries, reading, cycling, or researching music.
