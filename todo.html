Background

A design exploration for a classic to-do list app, where each task entry is intentionally kept short to help users focus on their tasks without unnecessary verbosity. Users can easily add and delete lists, maintaining a streamlined and efficient workflow.

Problem

I aimed to enhance my design and coding skills by creating a beautiful to-do app, ensuring a perfect 1:1 match between the design and the implemented code.

Solution

This straightforward to-do app is designed to be intuitive and efficient, focusing on two main functions: adding tasks and creating new to-do lists. What you see is what you get, ensuring a simple and user-friendly experience.
