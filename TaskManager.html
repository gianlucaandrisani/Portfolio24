Background

I have a passion for designing and using tools, whether they are for professional or everyday use. To challenge myself with a complex project, I decided to design and develop a new task manager that reflects my personal visual style and pushes my coding skills by implementing advanced features.

Problem

The main challenge was achieving high-quality UI implementation with precise 1:1 resemblance.

Solution

My task manager offers an elegant and discreet solution for managing personal tasks. I am currently expanding its functionality to include work tasks by integrating issue tracking tools. The ultimate goal is to provide users with a centralized hub to manage all their tasks across different software platforms.
