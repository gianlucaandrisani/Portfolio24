Seekuri

Problem

Children nowadays lack in exploration and outer playing modalities, threatening children's cognitive and creative development.

Solution

SeeKuri is a product-service that allows the child to explore the surrounding environment in total freedom, safety, guaranteed by the monitoring of the child by the parent, which is based on a strong concept of parent-child interaction, which goes to forge a bond and create memories that last over time.

Outcome

The solution has been pitched different times to young and new parents, seeking for validation and approval. Most of the sessions ended up with parents very interested and thrilled with our idea, stating how they would even buy the product.

Responsibilities

UX/UI Design, User Research,

Solution Iteration

For the goal of the project, different testing sessions were needed, for the app User Interface, Wearable UI and the communication between device and parent's app.

I tested both the app usability from an adult point of view and the device usability from the kid's side, but more effort and interest has been put in the usage scenarios between the app and the device, understanding which are the most important ones, coming through the importance of having the products directly communicating, making it possible to directly call the kid for the parent, but also to send a quick notification to check if the kid is ok.

Playful learning

Child and parent interact to choose and complete a game!

The whole gaming process will happen with continuous interaction between parent and kid, enhancing the bonding between the two and the chances for the kid to learn as much as possible.

The Design of the showed interface and the product that will follow has been chosen in order to provide a playful, entertaining and stimulating environment, to make children feel engaged and interested in the product and the activities.

This is why such a broad and colorful palette has been chosen to have a catchy and attentive interface not only to attract kids, but also to make parents feel sure and safe about what is happening when their child is far.

The of this system is to enhance the bonding and relationship between parent and their kid, as a matter of fact, most of the interactions and decisions that need to be taken within the app have to be taken together. The interactions are meant to last also after the game is over, going through the albums with the parent explaining the content to the child and also to be a tool all the moments and the explorations done together.

Key Takeaways
