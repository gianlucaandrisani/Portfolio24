WorkBuddy

Problem

People really get distracted by the usage of technology while they are meant to be productive, losing attention by Social media or Internet usage in general.
Also, may people struggle in organizational skills, not knowing how to properly schedule their tasks in order to be more proficient and to get most of the work done.

How to design an environment that helps people focus on their work to be done?

Solution

Workbuddy is your workmate and assistant who not only helps you not to get distracted, but organizes your work by recommending the best strategies and techniques to complete it!

Outcome

Developed the Web app of the MVP of the product by myself, published and shared amongst family and friends, whom are using it on a daily basis to help themselves to schedule and organizing their work, Also gathering feedbacks and insight for future improvements.

Try it yourself!

Responsibilities

UX/UI Design, User Research, Frontend Engineering

Solution iteration

Understood the most important information, then I went on trying to figure out how to make buddy a real work companion, one to interact with and to feel appreciated from, so I explored the interaction with the user wireframing the app and testing.

I tested the solution of making users more productive through Workbuddy, using a prototype of a timer passing by while their carrying on their work with a first iteration of Buddy coming up cheering and supporting the user while finishing the task.

When asking for users' feedback they were all very satisfied and pleased by the experience, appreciating how they felt Buddy's company and not feeling alone while working made them more focused and determined to complete the task.

Workroom

Boost users' productivity and avoid procrastination

Once the user starts working, will have access and enter to the Focus Workroom, with Buddy, your personal work assistant, in addition to being a companion, it will strive to create a pleasant, relaxing and creative environment.

These themes for the following feature have been chosen to create a relaxed and comfortable environment in which users can feel at their own ease, this is why the blue color.
Then the yellow has been chosen because very effective when used with blue on the color wheel and its rules, but specifically is the one which better fits the purpose of task completion and different CTAs because it is more attentive, focusing and energetic.

The final desired outcome of a comfortable environment which still lead to productivity and attention.

Buddy plans

Users let Buddy plan their schedule

The product will provide users with the chance to have their schedule fully planned by Buddy, their work companion.

This solution comes through the insight of users whom are unable to independently organize their time and task in order to be as most productive as possible.

Key Takeaways

Productivity can be a very subjective matter and what works for one person might not work effectively for another one, this means that some flows and interaction models can not be proficient for some specific users or some others prefer to manage their productivity on their own with their own techniques.

What could be improved

Design

Customization can be a great enhancement for the product, providing users with the tools for building their own techniques that might even refer to completely different models.

Engineering

React as a Front-end development technology can be sometimes constraining, with the one-pager app limitation I then decided to go around the problem implementing different modals that would perform the different actions, which can make the app heavier and slower. If I had to develop the product all over again I'd choose a different Front-end technology or to code in vanilla JavaScript.
